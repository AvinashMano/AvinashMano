Services

An experienced professional in this dual role blends strategic business analysis with agile project management to ensure teams build the right product, the right way. They are a facilitator, a planner, a problem-solver, and a communicator who bridges the gap between business stakeholders and the development team.

Requirements Elicitation & Management

Skillfully gathering, analyzing, documenting, and prioritizing requirements from stakeholders through interviews, workshops, and data analysis. This creates a clear and shared understanding of what needs to be built.

Stakeholder Collaboration

Acting as the primary liaison between business departments (like marketing, sales, and operations) and the technical team. This ensures business needs are accurately translated into technical specifications

Process Modeling & Analysis

Mapping current ("as-is") and future ("to-be") business processes to identify inefficiencies, gaps, and opportunities for improvement. This often involves creating diagrams like flowcharts and use case models.

Solution Design & Validation

Working with the development team to design solutions that meet business requirements. This includes validating that the proposed solution is feasible, practical, and aligns with strategic goals.

User Story & Acceptance Criteria Creation

Translating high-level business needs into detailed, actionable user stories and clear, testable acceptance criteria for the development team.

Impediment Removal

Identifying and removing any blockers or obstacles that are hindering the development team's progress. This could be anything from technical challenges to organizational hurdles.

Performance Tracking & Reporting

Monitoring and reporting on team progress using agile metrics like burndown charts, velocity, and cycle time.
